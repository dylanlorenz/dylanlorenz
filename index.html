Dylan Lorenz is a time-based media conservatorand audiovisual archivist based in New York City. He has worked at with the artist Amie Siegel, MLF Productions, the Educational Video Center, and has interned at the AFS Archives (within the AFS Intercultural Programs), NYU Fales Library, the film scanning and restoration house Cineric, Inc., and the Simon Preston Gallery. Most recently, he has been an Assistant Curator at the Denver Art Museum where he conserved electronic artwork in the Modern and Contemporary Art collection and assisted with preparing pieces scheduled for exhibition in 2019. He has an MA in Moving Image Archiving and Preservation from NYU.
